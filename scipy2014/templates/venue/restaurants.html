Restaurants

Nearby

  • Arturo's Casual, healthy breakfast and lunch spot with tacos, sandwiches, salads.
  • Flying Falafel & Po-Boys Fast, tasty, & affordable for lunch and dinner.
  • Hai Ky Vietnamese lunch spot, popular with SciPy attendees in the past.
  • Players American burger joint, popular with UT sports fans. Right outside the conference center!
  • El Mercado Nearby Mexican, can accommodate large parties.
  • The Carillon Situated in the AT&T Conference Center Hotel, the most convenient option for high-end dining.
  • Kerbey Lane Homegrown diner-style food. Open 24 hours.
  • Clay Pit Contemporary Indian. More formal atmosphere than most restaraunts in the area.
  • Ruby's BBQ Authentic Anthony Bourdain-approved Texan BBQ just north of campus.
  • Madam Mam's Fresh, spicy, and flavorful Thai cuisine.
  • Torchy's Tacos Award-winning hot sauce and inventive combinations. Tacos are an Austin essential.
  • Scholz Garten Historic biergarten with BBQ, German plates, and burgers.
  • Coco's Cafe Bubble tea and Taiwanese menu.
  • Veggie Heaven Vegetarian Asian cuisine; a student standby.
  • Trudy's, Hole in the Wall, Dog and Duck Favorite spots for drinks near campus.

Elsewhere

  • Stubb's Legendary BBQ and live music venue.
  • Uchi Exceptional Japanese fusion menu. One of the city's most acclaimed restaurants.
  • Polvo's, Guero's, Chuy's Popular takes on Tex-Mex.
  • Midori Sushi Reliably yummy sushi restaraunt in North Austin.
  • Mother's Cafe & Garden Vegan and vegetarian favorite. Great soups, enchiladas, veggie burgers and desserts.
  • Salt Lick Bar-B-Que A Hill Country Institution. BYOB family-style barbeque.
  • Madras Pavillion, Swad Two delectable vegetarian Indian spots in North Austin.
